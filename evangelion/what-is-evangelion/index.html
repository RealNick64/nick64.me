So, you want to watch Neon Genesis Evangelion. You may be asking "What is Neon Genesis Evangelion?"


What is it?

Neon Genesis Evangelion is a show created in 1995 by Gainax and directed by Hideaki Anno. The show is 26 episodes long and also has 2 movies (which I'll talk about later!). It's been revered by critics for its themes and characters, which I wholeheartedly agree with, it's really a show that makes you think a lot (maybe a little too much sometimes haha!).


Why is it good?

The show itself, while being a mecha anime like Mobile Suit Gundam Wing, has a lot more going for it with its religious undertones and focus on the charcter development of characters like Shinji and Asuka <3 (although Misato is cool too). It provides the viewer with a surreal experience that no other anime has done good yet (like Serial Experiments Lain, I'll talk about you later).
